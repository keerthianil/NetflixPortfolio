About Me

Hello, I'm Keerthi!

I'm that engineer who gets genuinely excited about both elegant code and its real-world impact. Currently pursuing my Master's at Northeastern, I wear multiple hats as a Teaching Assistant and Research Assistant— because why choose between mentoring future iOS developers and building accessible technology when you can do both?

My journey started at Capgemini, where I discovered that migrating 10+ enterprise applications to AWS wasn't just about the cloud—it was about transforming how businesses operate (and yes, saving on infrastructure costs makes everyone happy). From crafting cloud-native APIs to building iOS apps, I've learned that the best solutions are the ones users don't have to think about—they just work.

What sets me apart? I see the bigger picture in every line of code. When I implement Terraform modules, I'm thinking about the developers who'll use them six months from now. When I build an iOS feature, I'm obsessing over that split-second where frustration turns into delight. My expertise spans the full stack—AWS, Docker, Swift, CI/CD pipelines—but my real skill is knowing when to use each tool to create something that just works."
